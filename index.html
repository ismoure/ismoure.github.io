Isobel Moure is a researcher at the AI Disclosures Project

She received a BA in Cognitive and Computer Science from Barnard College in 2023. She worked for two years in the Barnard Speech Perception Lab and collaborated with the Ro Lab to complete her thesis analyzing EEG data for signs of syntactical discrimination.

She spent the 2023-2024 solo traveling throughout South America, practicing her mediocre Spanish, and climbing in the North Cascades when it wasn't raining or smokey.

She is interested in LLMs, AI ethics, computational linguistics, and learning more about emerging technologies. She lives in New York City.
